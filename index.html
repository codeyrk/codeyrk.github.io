Leadership Philosophy

How I think about building teams, platforms, and the culture that connects them.

I believe great engineering organizations are built around clarity ‚Äî clear ownership, clear standards, and a clear path for every engineer to grow. My job as a leader is to remove ambiguity, not add process. When teams know exactly what they own and why it matters, velocity follows naturally.
