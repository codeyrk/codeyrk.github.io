Leadership Philosophy

How I think about building teams, platforms, and the culture that connects them.

I believe great engineering organizations are built around clarity — clear ownership, clear standards, and a clear path for every engineer to grow. My job as a leader is to remove ambiguity, not add process. When teams know exactly what they own and why it matters, velocity follows naturally.

Technically, I gravitate toward platform thinking: investing in the foundations that give product teams superpowers. The fastest-moving orgs I've built weren't fast because people worked harder — they were fast because the platform made the right thing easy and the wrong thing obvious.
